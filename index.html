About Vewr

Vewr is a decentralized video-sharing platform that empowers creators and promotes freedom of expression. Our platform is built on cutting-edge blockchain technology, ensuring secure and censorship-resistant content hosting.

With Vewr, creators have full control over their content and can monetize it through various revenue-sharing models. Viewers can discover a diverse range of videos, enjoy personalized recommendations, and support their favorite creators.

  • Decentralized and censorship-resistant platform
  • Full control and monetization options for creators
  • Personalized video recommendations
  • Engaging community and interactive features
  • High-quality streaming and playback
